Why Programming?

As somebody who enjoys learning and experiencing new things, I have considered picking up programming on and off for few years now. Despite having having several family members in the field - I was still pretty hesitant. World of software engineering seemed very vast and profound, but after some deliberation I decided to make a full commitment to exploring and learning programming at Epicodus. As world is getting more technologically advanced, it's important to posses skills and abilities to work and contribute to our everchanging society and find ways to use software engineering to help those are in need.
